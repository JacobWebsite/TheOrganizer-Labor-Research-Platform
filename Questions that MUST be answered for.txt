Questions that MUST be answered for a gold standard research

1. What is the company name
	a. does it have alternative names
2. What is the parent company
	a. What companies are also owned by that company
	b. who are the investors in the company if they have investors?
3. What is the NAICS code, what do we infer the code down to 3, 4, 5, 6
4. What is the company address
	a. does it have other locations in the zip, MSA, state, country?
	b. What is the email address?
5. What type of company is it? Public, private, non-profit?
6. Who is the CEO of the parent company, who are the leadership team of the location?
7. Is there a union there?
	a. which union represents them? Are there multiple unions there?
	b. Has there ever been an NLRB election? What was the result?
	c. What are the most similar workplaces that are union?
8. How many people work there? 
	a. Are there online comments about what it's like to work there?
	b. How many job postings do they have at that location?
	c. What type of workers work there?
	d. What are the demographics of the workers in the area in the state, county, zip?
	e. How much do people make there?
	f. How much is the average wage in the industry, in the same occupations within the same state, county, zip
9.What is the revenue?
	a. Assets?
	b. liabilities?
10. Is the industry growing?
	a. Are the occupations in the industry growing?
	b. Are there many unions in the industry nationally, state, county, zip?

11. Does the employer receive federal, state, or municipal contracts? How much?
	a. Did they get PPP loans
	b. Do they register pension, healthcare, or other benefits with 5500 forms?
	c. do they have past osha violations?
	d. do they have national, state, or local labor violations?
12. What's the latest news on the company?
